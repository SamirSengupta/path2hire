Our Training Programs

Practical, Job-Oriented Modules Aligned with Industry. Each topic uses Skill2Hire's proven approach: hands-on, scenario-driven, and fully aligned with real business practices.

Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A)

Develop skills in budgeting, forecasting, variance analysis, financial modeling, and preparing insightful management reports with real company data.

Fund Accounting / Private Equity

Learn end-to-end fund accounting processes for investment firms, including capital calls, distributions, NAV calculations, and investor reporting.

IFRS

Apply International Financial Reporting Standards to practical scenarios, analyze financial statements, and bridge IFRS with GAAP through real-world examples.

KYC and AML

Work through customer onboarding, due diligence, and fraud detection procedures using up-to-date compliance case studies.

Order to Cash (O2C)

Simulate the full O2C lifecycle: order management, billing, collections, cash application, and dispute handling as seen in global organizations.

Procure to Pay (P2P)

Process end-to-end procurement scenarios, from purchase requisition to invoice processing and vendor payments, reinforcing compliance and controls.

Record to Report (R2R)

Experience the complete financial close process, balance sheet reconciliation, journal entries, and management reporting.

UK GAAP

Tackle UK GAAP requirements by preparing and analyzing reports, supported by real business situations and compliance exercises.

US GAAP

Practice interpreting and applying US Generally Accepted Accounting Principles on real-life company scenarios.
