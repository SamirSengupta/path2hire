Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Path2Hire ("we", "us", "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

2. Information We Collect

a. Information You Provide

When you create an account, take an assessment, upload a resume, or contact us, we collect:

  • Name, email address, phone number (if provided)
  • Resume/CV files
  • Assessment answers and scores
  • Any messages you send

b. Automatic/Technical Data

We collect session cookies and technical logs necessary for operation and security:

  • IP address, browser user-agent, timestamps
  • Activity logs
  • Session cookies to keep you logged in and preserve assessment progress

c. Job Listing Metadata

Our job-scraper fetches public job postings (title, company, location, description excerpt, and a link) to display within the app. We do not claim ownership of scraped listings—they link back to original sources.

3. How We Use Information

We use data to:

  • Provide and operate the Service (generate career reports, show jobs, deliver downloads)
  • Improve the Service and personalize recommendations
  • Communicate with you (email updates, support)
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect the Service

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

Service Providers

We may use hosting providers, analytics, and email services. They have access only to the data necessary to perform their functions.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law.

No Sale of Personal Data

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

5. Storage and Security

We store data on our servers and follow reasonable security practices (hashed passwords, HTTPS recommended in production). However, no service is 100% secure. If we detect a breach, we'll comply with applicable notification laws.

6. Your Choices

Access & Export

You may request a copy of your personal data (assessment results, uploaded files).

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and personal data. We will process per our deletion policy (note: backups may persist for a limited time).

Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser, but site functionality (assessment session, login) may be affected.

7. Children

The Service is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 provided us personal data, contact us for deletion.

8. Third-Party Content

We link to and load external assets (Google Fonts, Font Awesome). These providers may collect usage data per their own privacy terms.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We'll post changes here with a revised effective date.
