Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often

How long do your training programs take?

Our programs typically range from 4-8 weeks depending on the specialization. We offer both intensive full-time and flexible part-time schedules to accommodate working professionals.

Do I need prior experience to enroll?

No prior experience is required! Our programs are designed for career changers and fresh graduates. We start with fundamentals and build up to advanced, job-ready skills.

What are the payment options for your programs?

We offer flexible payment plans, including installments and scholarships for eligible candidates. Contact our admissions team for detailed information on pricing and financial aid.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Absolutely! Our dedicated career services team provides resume building, interview preparation, and connects you with our extensive network of hiring partners to help you land your dream job.
