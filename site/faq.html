All Your Questions Answered

Common Questions

What is Path2Hire?

Path2Hire is a career assessment and job-discovery platform that helps users discover strengths, generate personalized career reports, and find relevant job openings.

How does the career assessment work?

The assessment asks behavior-focused questions and maps answers to a set of career attributes. These attribute scores are converted into a readable career blueprint highlighting your strengths, areas to develop, suggested roles, and next steps.

Is my data private?

Yes. We store only the information you submit (name, email, phone, assessment answers, uploaded resume/CV). We don't sell your data. You can request account export or deletion. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Can I download my career report?

Yes. After you complete the assessment, we generate a downloadable report. Admin users can download aggregated exports for permitted purposes.

Where do the job listings come from?

Job listings are collected by our job-scraper module from public job boards and employer websites to help you find opportunities. Each listing links back to the source so you can apply on the original site.

Can I remove my uploaded resume or delete my account?

Yes. Contact support or use the account settings page if available. We also provide an admin route to purge uploaded career data for testing or admin cleanup. User-led deletions are honored per the Privacy Policy.

Are third-party libraries or external services used?

The site loads third-party front-end assets (e.g., Google Fonts, Tailwind, Font Awesome). For job data we parse public listings. We document third-party usage in our privacy policy.

Who can access my information?

You (the account owner) and authorized Path2Hire administrators. We log admin activities and keep access restricted.

Is Path2Hire free?

The core assessment and report generation is free. Paid or premium features (if any) will be clearly labeled.

Who do I contact for help?

Email the support address in the footer or use the contact form. If you want immediate removal of personal data, include your email and request deletion.
